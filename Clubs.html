Clubs

Muslims Student Assoc. (MSA)

Diversity is what makes Canada strong. As a Muslim, my responsibility is to spread peace and promote equity and inclusivity in my community. I joined my school's Muslim Student Association to promote understanding, raise awareness of the Islamic culture, build cross-religion bridges, and cultivate a sense of belonging to new Muslim students. As the president, I served the needs of 200+ Muslim students and facilitated social engagement in safe and accommodating environments. With my team, the MSA successfully ran a plethora of events and global-issue fundraisers, including our yearly Ramadan Iftar, Relief for the Middle East Crisis fundraiser and our international Hijab Day events!

Robotics Clubs

I believe I was born with a STEM instinct. I built robots as young as 8 years old. I established (or helped to flourish) robotics clubs in all my previous schools in Canada and the USA. High school was no exception. As president of my schools robotics club, I strive to design and create complex and challenging objects. As well, myself and my team engage in a plethora of competitions, including the Ontario Tech Sumo Bot Challenge and the Alamein International Robotics Championship in Egypt. Working with my team, I have gained extraordinary skills in the art of robotics and formed a lasting bond with my team that will persist for years to come.

Student Council

As my school's student council leadership minister, I strive to cultivate an environment that fosters creativity and nurtures personal and professional development. I organized leadership training workshops and fundraising events to raise awareness for global issues. I orchestrated with other clubs to organize cultural days. I established events to welcome, orient, and integrate the 9th graders into high school society. I communicated with students from all grades to better connect with the student body. I am grateful that I was able to sharpen my communication skills, demonstrate leadership by example, voice our students' concerns, and serve our school community.

Construction SHSM

As somebody who is fond of design and construction, I love to create designs and constructions and I am always looking for opportunities to put my skills into actions. I joined my school's construction SHSM program, where I learned about the trades and construction. Our team was tasked to design and build a goat shelter for the Oshawa Zoo. My creative eco-friendly design won first place and I led the team in building and installing the project. The design considered several factors for the goats to exhibit natural behavior such as comfort, safety, and the visitors' viewing experience. I am glad that I was able to benefit my community.
